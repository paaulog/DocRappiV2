Payless

Integrated payment model in which Rappi manages billing to the end customer.

Requirements for Payless integration

To operate with the Payless model at Rappi, there are some important points:

  • Integrated inventory.
  • Valid Bearer credentials.
  • Active Payless commercial agreement with Rappi.

Relevant flows

Sales flow: customer pays on Rappi → Rappi consolidates amounts and transfers to the partner according to the contract.

Financial reconciliation flow: use periodic reports to verify transfers.

Guide

Payless and Open Orders are exclusive — each partner adopts only one.

Do not request additional payment at delivery: the customer has already paid through the platform.

Monitor the financial cycle with the Rappi commercial team to avoid discrepancies.
