Loyalty

Loyalty program tied to purchases made through Rappi.

Requirements for loyalty program

To integrate the loyalty program in Rappi, there are some important points:

  • Open Orders or Payless already in production.
  • Valid Bearer credentials.
  • Scoring and redemption rules defined with the commercial team.

Relevant flows

Scoring flow: for each eligible purchase, register the points assigned to the customer.

Redemption flow: validate balance, debit points, and confirm the released benefit.

Guide

Loyalty depends on reliable order history — therefore it assumes Open Orders or Payless.

Clearly communicate rules (validity, fees, exclusions) to the end customer.

Monitor redemption rates to adjust the program economics.
